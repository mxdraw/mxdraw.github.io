How to Install watchOS 5 Beta 6 on Apple Watch without Developer Account

There are quite a few folks who would like to download and install watchOS 5 beta 6 on your Apple Watch even without developer account just to play around with the all new features. If you also belong to the same group, head over to get the latest watchOS version on your smartwatch!

By Jignesh Padhiyar
Read now

So, you have been charmed by some of the coolest features of watchOS 5 and wish to give it a try sooner than later despite knowing the fact that the OS is currently available only for developers? To keep the mystery out of the way right from the word go, let me tell you that there is a quick way to install watchOS beta 6 on Apple Watch even without dev account!

Talking about the process, it’s quite simple. All you have to do is track down a watchOS beta profile and download it on your iPhone. From there onwards, it’s business as usual. Just a quick reminder, don’t expect the OS to be stable as yet. Therefore, you should be prepared to tackle some issues that may crop up.

Update: If you had already installed the previous watchOS beta, updating it is pretty simple. Just head to the Watch app on your paired iPhone General Software Update and then install the update as usual. But if you are yet to get started with the dev beta, follow the steps mentioned below.

How to Download and Install watchOS 5 Beta 6 on Apple Watch without Developer Account

Requirements

  • Be sure your Watch is compatible with watchOS 5 that doesn’t support the first gen Apple Watches.
  • Your iPhone must be runningiOS 12 beta 6.
  • Make sure your device is connected to Wi-Fi network.
  • The wearable device must have at least 50% battery life.

As mentioned above, first off we need to download the watchOS beta profile.

Step #1.Launch Safari on your paired iPhone and visit this site.

Step #2.Now, tap on Slow Download option.

Step #3. Allow website to make changes. Next tap on Apple Watch.

Sponsored Links

Next up, the Watch app will open on the iPhone. You need to tap on Install at the top right Enter your iPhone Passcode.

Then, tap on Install from the top right corner and tap on Install again at the bottom to confirm.

Step #4.Tap on Restart in the popup to reboot your Apple Watch.

Step #5. Once the process is completed, tap onDoneat the top right corner of your iPhone.

Now, head to the Software Update section to finish the installation process.

How to Install watchOS 5 Beta on Apple Watch

Step #1.Open Watchappon your iPhone.

Step #2. Tap on My Watch at the bottom Tap onGeneral Tap on Software Update.

Step #3.Next up, tap onDownload & Install and enter your iPhone passcode Up next, tap on Agree to accept Apple’s Terms and Conditions.

That’s done!

Now, you will have to wait for a while until the process is completed successfully.

Get Started

Once you have got the watchOS 5 live on your smartwatch, go ahead and dig into everything that has caught your eyes. But don’t fail to share your excitement with us and the things that you have liked a lot in the newest iteration of the watchOS.
